Motivation

Following the completion of my BSc in Business Engineering and my current position in the SBB, my field of expertise has crystallized around data design and data visualization. Through the MSc program I aim to expand my knowledge in the data science field and gain all necessary skills to continue a successful career in the interface between people, data and design.

In my current role as an enterprise architect, I am often faced with the challenge of pointing out future changes. For this purpose, decision makers have to be convinced of the urgency with a focused and conclusive communication. Only then can the necessary projects be launched to remain also competitive in the future. Today, we operate in an increasingly data-based and complex environment that is often difficult to understand. Even though I haven't been working in my current role for very long, I can already see that important projects are stopped or not even started due to a lack of support from management. Not always, but very often in my opinion, it is due to a lack of communication and poorly prepared data. This fact already motivated me to visit the DAS in Data Science. With the CAS in DataViz I found my passion in the field of information and data visualisation.

I am convinced that with the rising data amount, we are more than ever required to combine data analysis skills with a creative data driven design approach to make better decisions.

The interface between data, people and design remain my passion and chosen field of specialization. The achievement of deepen my know-how in the wide field of data science is my motivation and ultimate goal for the completion of this master’s degree.

The MSc in Applied Information and Data Science best reflects my development goals and I would greatly appreciate the opportunity to expand my knowledge base through this program.
